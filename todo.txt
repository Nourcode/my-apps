Feature suggestions

Based on everything built so far, here are the ones I think would add real value:

1. App status — Active / Trial / Cancelled
You could mark an app as cancelled without removing it. Cancelled apps show greyed out or in a collapsed "Archive" section. Useful for tracking what you've tried and why you stopped, especially with the notes field now available.

2. Upcoming billing alerts
Since you already track billing dates, cards could show a small amber dot or "due in 3 days" label when a payment is within 7 days. Requires no new data — just a date comparison at render time.

3. Pin / prioritize apps
Let certain apps always appear first in their category. The apps you open daily (Discord, Gmail) shouldn't need scrolling past ones you barely use.

4. Keyboard shortcut for search
Press / or Cmd+K anywhere on the page to focus the main search bar. Standard in any tool people use frequently — no UI change needed, just a keydown listener.

5. Trial expiry tracking
A "Trial ends" date field in the payment sechows a countdown badge on the card. Extremely easy to forget free trials until you get charged.

6. Account field
Which email you use for each app. A lot of paccounts and mix them up. One small textfield in the modal, shown in the tooltip.

7. Export to calendar
Generate an .ics file from all billing datesndar / Google Calendar as recurringreminders. Zero infrastructure needed — pure client-side file generation.

8. Cost breakdown view
A dedicated panel or slide-out that shows: tost per category ranked, and free vs paidratio. You have all the data already — it's just a different presentation of what's in the stats bar.                 
The ones I'd prioritize first: billing alerts (highest impact, zero new data needed), app status (natural extension of the payment system), and keyboard shortcut (aily use).
